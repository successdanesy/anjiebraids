Before You Book

Please review the following Guidelines carefully before proceeding to book your appointment.

💰 A $15 deposit is required to secure an appointment.

The deposit secures your slot under certain conditions.
Currently, we are only accepting deposits via Interac e-Transfer. Card payments will resume soon.

📌 No braiding hair is provided.

You must bring your own. If not pre-stretched, drop it off a day early.

📦 Don’t book if your extension isn’t ready.

Ensure your braiding hair is ready before booking to avoid last-minute issues.

✂️ Only pre-stretched extensions are used.

Additional charges may apply if your extensions are not pre-stretched.

🧼 Hair must be clean and dry.

No oils or creams — come with fresh, clean hair for best results.

⏰ Don’t be late.

Your appointment will be canceled 30 minutes after the scheduled time if you're late without a refund.

❌ Cancel 48 hours in advance for a refund.

Refunds are only available for cancellations made 48 hours before the appointment.

👥 Guests are allowed (not children).

You may bring a guest for support, but not children during your session.

Got questions? Just ask.
Clarify anything with me ahead of time to avoid confusion.
